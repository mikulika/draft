ABOUT THIS APPLICATION

Maktab is your one-stop destination for personalized learning tailored to your academic journey. With Maktab, students can seamlessly navigate through a variety of school subjects, selecting their grade level and desired subjects to embark on an enriching learning experience.

From mathematics to literature, science to history, Maktab offers a comprehensive array of courses aligned with the Uzbekistan curriculum. With just a few clicks, students can choose their grade and subject, unlocking a wealth of resources, interactive lessons, and practice exercises designed to enhance their understanding and mastery of key concepts.
